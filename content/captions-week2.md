## Day 11 — Slot 2 (T-B) — `img/t-b-uji.jpg`

**Caption:**
The matcha journey:

Picked in late spring in Uji. Steamed within hours to lock the green. Air-dried, de-stemmed, slow-ground between granite stones for two hours per 30 grams. Sealed under nitrogen. Shipped to us in nitrogen-flushed pouches.

By the time it lands in a jar, it has done more travelling than most of us.

We use it the way it was grown to be used: gently, fully, and never as a colourant.

#ujimatcha #ceremonialmatcha #firstharvest #japanesetea #matchasourcing #craftsmanship #matchalove #tealover

## Day 12 — Slot 2 (T-C) — `img/t-c-toast.jpg`

**Caption:**
A breakfast routine that took us a while to get right:

- Toast the sourdough fully (not pale).
- Wait 30 seconds — you want it warm, not hot, otherwise the spread goes runny.
- A generous layer of Aoba, not a polite one.
- A small handful of berries if you've got them.

Kids ask for seconds. Parents drink their coffee in peace. Nobody negotiates.
